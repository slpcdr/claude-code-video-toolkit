# Digital Samba Context

## What We Are
Digital Samba is a European video conferencing platform, founded in 2003. We provide embeddable video conferencing infrastructure for developers and businesses.

## Core Product: Digital Samba Embedded
White-label video conferencing API and SDK. Developers embed our technology into their own applications - telehealth platforms, EdTech, customer support tools, virtual events, etc.

## Key Selling Points
- **Privacy-first**: EU-hosted, GDPR-native, zero tracking
- **Developer-friendly**: Embed with minimal code
- **20+ years experience**: Reliable, battle-tested infrastructure
- **White-label**: Fully customisable to match client branding

## Brand Voice
- Approachable experts - knowledgeable without ego
- Honest and direct - no marketing fluff
- Confident but not salesy
- Human and conversational

## Tone Guidelines

### Do
- Speak like a helpful colleague, not a corporate brochure
- Focus on what users/developers can achieve
- Be specific and practical
- Use "you" and "your"

### Don't
- Use buzzwords: "leverage", "cutting-edge", "seamless", "world-class"
- Oversell or hype
- Sound robotic or overly formal
- Make vague claims without substance

## Example Phrasing
- ❌ "Enterprise-grade solution with seamless integration"
- ✅ "Add video calls to your app in an afternoon"

- ❌ "Industry-leading security features"
- ✅ "Your data stays in Europe. No tracking, no ads."
